Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity and size of the website. Basic websites typically take 2-3 weeks, while more advanced custom solutions can take 4-6 weeks or more.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing support and maintenance services to ensure your website remains up-to-date, secure, and fully functional.

What is SEO, and do you offer it with web development?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps improve your website's visibility on search engines like Google. SEO is included in all of our web development packages to ensure your site ranks well and attracts traffic.

Do you offer e-commerce solutions?

Yes, we offer advanced e-commerce solutions that include shopping carts, payment gateway integration, product catalogs, and more to help you run your online store efficiently.

Do you offer digital marketing services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive digital marketing services, including SEO, social media marketing, PPC campaigns, and email marketing to help promote your website and business.

What is the difference between a WordPress website and a custom website?

A WordPress website is built using pre-designed templates and themes, making it a quicker and more affordable solution for simple sites. It's ideal for blogs, small businesses, and personal websites. On the other hand, a custom website is fully tailored to your specific requirements, offering unique design, advanced functionality, and greater flexibility. Custom websites are typically better suited for businesses that need complex features or a highly personalized user experience.
